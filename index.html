About

My Journey from B.A. to Cybersecurity to iOS Development

  • 2017

    B.A. Communication

    Graduated from UMD with a B.A. in Communication and a 3.5 gpa. During my time here, I realized that I wanted to move into something more technical

  • 2017-2019

    M.P.S. Cybersecurity

    Graduated with a 4.0 gpa as Phi Kappa Phi for placing in top 10% of graduate students that year. It was during my time here while learning about NIST documentation and best practices that I started to dive into iOS and mobile security.

  • 2019

    Mobile Security Thesis Paper with IoT & iOS

    I wrote my thesis paper on mobile security regarding a popular home security IoT device alongside its iOS app used to faciliate communication. I ultimately built a simplified version of an app I proposed to help secure IoT devices. The app utilized various pods sourced from Github to determine when an IoT device on the network went offline.

  • June 2019 - Current

    Xcode 10 + Swift 4.2 / 5

    With roughly one year of Swift 4.2 and Xcode 10 under my belt, I have been working to build up my portfolio with examples that demonstrate my current understanding of Swift and Xcode. I will conitnue to add to my portfolio, and am currently seeking a Jr. iOS Developer posisition.
